Transportation

From Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)

1. MRT: Airport Terminal Station (A12 or A13) to Taoyuan HSR Station (A18)

  • Timetable
  • Fare: NT$ 25

2. Taiwan High Speed Rail: Taoyuan to Tainan

  • Timetable
  • Fare: NT$ 1,190

3. Train: Shalun Station (4272) to Tainan Sation (4220)

  • Timetable
  • Fare: NT$ 25

4. Tainan Sation to National Cheng Kung University

  • Please exit by Rear Exit. And then Go along Daxue Road. When you see the school gate, please turn left. After entering the school gate, please turn left at the next intersection. And you can find an International conference room. It takes around 7 minutes to walk.
